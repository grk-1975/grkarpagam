G R Karpagam

Professor, Department of Computer Science & Engineering

PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, India

Director, PSG AI Consortium  ·  Senior IEEE Member  ·  ACM Vice Chair

Dr. G. R. Karpagam is a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, with over 29 years of academic, research and industry-engagement experience. She received her B.E. (1996, Bharathiar University), M.E. (2001, PSG College of Technology) and Ph.D. (2008, Anna University) — all in Computer Science and Engineering.

Her research spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, LLMs, Explainable AI, Federated Learning, Blockchain, Service-Oriented Architecture, Cloud Computing and Cyber Security & Privacy. She has established nine state-of-the-art laboratories through government and industry partnerships (DST, DBT, AICTE, Cognizant, Cordys, Impiger, HPE, Siemens).

As Director of the PSG AI Consortium (est. February 2025), she leads a flagship AI Wing housing four research centres. She organised AI Spectrum 2025 — a 3-day international conference officially recognised as a Pre-Summit Event of the India AI Impact Summit 2026 (MeitY, Govt. of India), with Springer proceedings.

She also serves as Professor In-Charge, Library (since 2019), spearheading library automation, mobile app development and RFID-based systems at PSG College of Technology.

PSG AI Consortium, AI Wing & AI Spectrum 2025 Director

PSG AI Consortium — Established February 2025

The PSG AI Consortium was established at PSG College of Technology with a clear mandate: to shape the future of responsible, inclusive and impactful Artificial Intelligence. Members were nominated by the Patron from all departments, forming a truly interdisciplinary body aligned with AICTE's academic and societal vision. In under ten months, the Consortium evolved from inception to impact — building a strong institutional AI ecosystem and setting the foundation for its flagship event, AI Spectrum 2025, officially recognised as a Pre-Summit Event of the India AI Summit 2026.

WDC @ PSG College of Technology — Established 2015

The Women Development Cell was established in 2015, and Dr. G. R. Karpagam was nominated as its first Faculty Advisor. The Cell is committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment that nurtures the physical, emotional, intellectual and social well-being of women on campus.

Motto: 3E — Encourage, Enhance and Empower

Objectives

  • To create awareness of feminine potential in terms of social, ethical and gender equality for overall personality development
  • To highlight the importance of health and hygiene
  • To organize seminars, workshops and interactive sessions relating to women development

Mission

Build self-esteem and assertiveness  ·  Cultivate leadership qualities  ·  Promote effective time management  ·  Enhance safety awareness and self-defence training  ·  Develop vital life skills for resilience and independence
